# RBAC vs ACL

## Why do we need Security in ADLS Gen2?

Organizations store sensitive data in ADLS Gen2 such as customer information, employee records, financial transactions, medical reports, and business documents.

Not every user should have access to all the data. Different users require different levels of access based on their roles and responsibilities.

To control who can access the data and what actions they can perform, ADLS Gen2 provides security features such as Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) and Access Control Lists (ACLs).
